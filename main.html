Kochi


Kochi, Kerala, is famous for its Cherai Beach. Guests should not miss seeing this beach as it features picturesque scenery and a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation. Aside from this, guests can also visit the beautiful Mattancherry Palace. It is a historical place that showcases the city's local history.
